The artist

Portrait
Photograph pending

Sittiphon Lochaisong — known to friends and collectors as Bomb — is a Thai painter born in 1991 in Maha Sarakham, Thailand. He holds both a BFA and an MFA in Visual Arts from Silpakorn University.

His practice centers on tempera powder on canvas, a material he has returned to across a body of work spanning solo exhibitions in Thailand, Malaysia, and Italy. Idealistic Universe (2017), the twelve-part series presented in the gallery above, remains a touchstone in that practice.
